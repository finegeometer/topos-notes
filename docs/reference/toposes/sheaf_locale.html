Sheaves on a locale

Let L be a locale.

Sh(L) classifies the theory of completely prime filters on O(L). This is equivalently the theory of points of L.

​∀A∈O(L),∀A≤B,∀A,B∈O(L),∀B⊆O(L),​AA,BB∈B⋁​B​⊢A:Prop⊢B⊢⊤⊢A∧B⊢B∈B⋁​B​

See also

Generalizations:

  • Sheaves on a posite

Proof

We wish to find the theory that Sh(L) classifies.

Sheaves over a locale are a special case of sheaves over a posite, so let's start there. ​∀A∈P,∀A≤B,∀A,B∈P,∀A◃B,​AA,BA​⊢A:Prop⊢B⊢A∈P⋁​A⊢C≤A,B⋁​C⊢B∈B⋁​B​

We specialize to the case of locales. Since P=O(L) is a meet-lattice, we can simplify rules (3) and (4). ​∀A∈O(L),∀A≤B,∀A,B∈O(L),∀B⊆O(L),​AA,BB∈B⋁​B​⊢A:Prop⊢B⊢⊤⊢A∧B⊢B∈B⋁​B​
