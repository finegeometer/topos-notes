Weighted 2-Colimits

The 2-category of toposes is supposedly cocomplete. But do not yet know how to construct colimits.

So let's analyze a special case.

Coproducts

Let {Ti​∣i∈I} be a collection of theories. Then the coproduct of the classifying topoi, i∈I∐​Set[Ti​], classifies the following theory. ​∀i∈I,∀i,j∈I,∀i∈I,​Pi​,Pj​Pi​​⊢Pi​:Prop⊢i∈I⋁​Pi​⊢i=j⊢Ti​​

Proof: ​This theory=(P:i∈I∐​Prop)×(i∈I⋁​Pi​)×(i,j∈I⋀​Pi​∧Pj​→i=j)×(i∈I∏​Pi​→Ti​)=(P:i∈I∐​Type)×(i∈I∏​(x,y:Pi​)→x=y)×(i∈I⋁​Pi​)×(i,j∈I∏​Pi​∧Pj​→i=j)×(i∈I∏​Pi​→Ti​)=(P:i∈I∐​Type)×((x,y:i∈I∐​Pi​)→x=y)×(i∈I⋁​Pi​)×(i∈I∏​Pi​→Ti​)=(ΣP:Type)×(f:ΣP→I)×((x,y:ΣP)→x=y)×(∃x:ΣP.⊤)×((x:ΣP)→Tf(x)​)=(ΣP:Prop)×(f:ΣP→I)×ΣP×((x:ΣP)→Tf(x)​)=(f:1→I)×((x:1)→Tf(x)​)=(i:I)×Ti​=i∈I∐​Ti​​

And as discussed here, the colimit diagram for these theories lifts to a colimit diagram in topoi.
